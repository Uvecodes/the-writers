Meet Our Founder

Timbuk John Inyang is the Founder of The Writer's Manger Network. He is a graduate of Accounting from The University of Uyo and holds a Master's degree in Business Administration from Franklin University, Columbus, Ohio, United States of America. He is the Author of the Anthology, "Remember me Black child and other Poems"; a collection which is part of the reading catalogue at Columbus Metropolitan Library, USA. He is passionate about promoting literacy and creative writing, and spends his spare time reading, writing, and playing music.
